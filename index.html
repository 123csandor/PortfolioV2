About me




Hello, my name is Christopher, but you can call me CJ. I have a strong passion for web designing and web development. I enjoy the process of building websites from the ground up, with a strong intent of doing my best. Each new website I create is always better than the last, only getting better with time. I feel that many websites are poorly developed, many don’t have any support for the disabled, or “too complex” design for the elderly. Websites should be a fast and smooth experience to get the information you need, not a maze to get to a small page.




I offer a simple and easy process to make these simple, but well-designed websites. The building process would offer different layouts/designs for you to choose from. Themes are very important to a website and you would choose very wisely. Then there is the hardest part the development part. Coding with HTML and CSS feels very easy, simple, and has never held back any design I’ve had before. I offer many options and designs to give you the best simple website you could ever need.
